Privacy, by design.

Your data stays
exactly where it belongs.

DevSuite was built from day one with one rule — nothing leaves your machine. Not your code. Not your secrets. Not your usage patterns.

No cloud, ever

Every tool runs entirely in your browser. No servers receive your data. No uploads. No API calls to external services. Everything stays local.

No accounts needed

DevSuite requires no sign-up, no login, no license key, and no email address. Open the page and start working immediately.

AES-256 encrypted

Where data is stored locally — Secret Vault, DevDB Manager — it is protected with AES-256-GCM encryption and BLAKE2b integrity checks.
